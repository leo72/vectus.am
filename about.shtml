Who We Are

Vectus LLC was founded on 12 March, 2019 in Yerevan, Armenia. The company founders decided to unite their team of senior level programmers and professionals around the idea of web architecture development service provision based on the state of the art technologies with a specific focus on Drupal platform and web engineering toolsets.

The Vectus team is dedicated to implement the company mission, as a separate business, asserting into the daily work their advanced knowledge and professional experience accumulated while working in different IT companies and smart projects.

We are committed to developing relationships and ensuring success to the business of our customers. Vectus LLC adopted values aimed at acting as accountable customer-centric company with highest standards of integrity in our responsible actions and service portfolio provision.

Our mission is to provide a portfolio of high quality, innovative and reliable solutions for private and corporate sector. Not only we build professional, mobile-optimized websites to help you grow your business, but we also become your partner for all your online needs.

We are able to make impossible things possible by following a strategic approach.

1. Discover

In this phase, we ask you significant questions like:
What is the intention of creating a website? What are you looking forward from the website? What is the ultimate goal of your website? What sort of audience do you want to target? Who are your competitors and their websites? What are your basic requirements? What is your time-limit? What is your fiscal estimate?

2. Plan

The secret of the success is determined by your daily agenda. We are sure about the fact that planning makes the whole process and the implementation part easier and without complications. This phase involves developing the graphical structure of the website, sketching wire frames, content sketches, functional sketches, website flowcharts, page divisions etc.

3. Design

“Design can be art. Design can be simple. That’s why it’s so complicated.” - Paul Rand

We completely understand that designing a project is like designing a new home. After an in-depth research of the newfangled trends, we choose the astonishing and eye-catching color combinations, typography, partitioning of the page content, videos, animations, and images etc.

4. Develop

We are develop flawless and perfect dream projects for the clients by using the skills of most talented adept and advance-guard technology. The approval of design on your side is the first-step. Our development team of experts then starts the process and show the magic by transforming the layout into an awesome reality. The testing of the website is also done in this phase by running the website prototype on major web browsers for checking the total functionality and response time of the web pages.

5. Launch

One of the most significant tasks is launching a website. After the prototype is approved by you, the website is ready to be consumed by the search engines. The content is checked many times for any kinds of flaws including grammar or spellings etc . We make sure that your audience gets the ultimate web experience. We believe a launch is like the beginning of a sprint race i.e. better the beginning, higher are the chances of success.

6. Maintenance

Our services don’t end after the launch of your website. All of our projects include a complimentary 40-day Service Level Agreement (SLA). This covers bug fixes and site issues that may arise immediately following launch. For clients looking for continuous support and improvements we keep the site up-to-date on an ongoing basis but also includes scheduled meetings and planning each month for defining specific areas of focus.
